## What I Do

I architect and develop software systems at the intersection of **industrial automation** and **modern web development**. My work spans the full stack: from PLC integration and SCADA systems on the plant floor to TypeScript microservices and Next.js applications in the cloud.

### Industrial Automation & Manufacturing
- **Ignition Platform** — SCADA, MES, and reporting applications for distillery operations
- **Warehouse Management Systems** — Full-stack WMS with Android scanning, QR tracking, and ERP integration
- **TTB Compliance** — Regulatory compliance systems for spirits production
- **Network Topology & Connectivity** — Industrial network discovery, mapping, and monitoring

### AI & Agentic Systems
- **RAG for Industrial Codebases** — [ignition-rag-agent](https://github.com/pmannion2/ignition-rag-agent): Retrieval-augmented generation for Ignition projects with IDE integration
- **Multi-Agent Analytics** — Postgres data analytics using agentic architectures
- **AI in Manufacturing** — [ICC 2025 Speaker](https://github.com/WhiskeyHouse/icc-ai-in-manufacturing-2025): Containerized Ignition and AI-based development workflows

### Full-Stack Development
- **Frontend** — Next.js, React, TypeScript, Tailwind
- **Backend** — Node.js, Python, FastAPI, GraphQL (DGS)
- **Mobile** — Kotlin/Android for industrial scanning applications
- **Infrastructure** — Docker, Azure Functions, n8n workflows, Let's Encrypt automation
